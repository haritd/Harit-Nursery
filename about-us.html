ABOUt US

HARIT NURSERY is a specialist wholesale and consumers centric nursery specially environment awareness through the campaigning and conserving the environment located in Allahabad Uttar Pradesh India..

We offer our many service and product to clients & customer throughout the India, whether you are an Individual to imbue a tried garden with new life or a commercial enterprise complementing your existing premises with an exciting new landscaping project.

We work hard to supply thousands of plants, shrubs, tree, topiary, and medicinal plant all sourced from the most of our production in nursery and distinguished growers in the India.

You can review our portfolio while we collate a bespoke plant selection. We can then create an edit that gives you a taste of what the final layout and plant selection will feel like in your garden. Over our 30 years in the industry, our dedicated team have regularly transformed the green spaces of small, urban terrace gardens in India.

We believe in providing our clients with luxury gardens, landscapes and an escape from the built environment. We are also a very approachable team who pride ourselves on our high standards of service, amenability and sensitivity to your home or business.
